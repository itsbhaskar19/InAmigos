Who We Are

Tour To Our Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is more than an NGO — it's a movement powered by passionate young minds committed to social change.

InAmigos Foundation is a NGO licensed by the Central Government and registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, with its base at Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh. It was founded on 23rd September 2020 and started working for the betterment of society.

We operate across multiple verticals — from food drives and cleanliness campaigns to digital empowerment programs — always putting community first.

Our Corporate Identity Number is U85300CT2020NPL010641. We are 80G and 12A certified by the Commissioner of Income Tax, ensuring full financial transparency.

ISO 9001:2015 Certified NGO DARPAN – NITI Aayog CSR-1 Registered 80G & 12A Certified Trademarked Brand MCA Registered

COVID-19 Relief Work

When the pandemic hit, InAmigos Foundation stood up for the community. From distributing food to visiting orphanages, we were on the ground when it mattered most.

➤ Distributed food to over 1500+ people during the COVID-19 lockdown
➤ Distributed stationery items to students in Kolkata

Transparency

Where Your Funds Go

Every rupee is accounted for. Here's exactly how donations are utilized to create lasting impact.

🍱 Food Drives

Daily meal distribution in slum areas, nutritional kits for pregnant women, dry ration kits for low-income families, and "no food waste" campaigns.

🤝 Social Work

Sponsoring education for underprivileged children (books, uniforms, fees), free health camps, and vocational training for youth and women.

🎁 Charity Drives

Providing winter clothes and blankets to homeless individuals, supporting medical emergencies, and donating school supplies to students in need.

🧹 Cleanliness Drives

Street cleaning campaigns, installing dustbins in public areas, and promoting plastic-free zones by distributing cloth and jute bags.

💻 Technical Costs

Website hosting, domain costs, maintenance, updates, and online payment gateways for secure donation collection.

📋 Compliance & Operations

Monthly GST filing, annual auditing, legal filings, utility bills, stipends for volunteers and interns, and community outreach programs.
